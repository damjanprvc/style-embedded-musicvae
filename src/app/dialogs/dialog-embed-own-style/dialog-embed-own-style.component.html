Embed Your Own Style

Upload some melodies that define your style.

The melodies will be cut in 2-, 4- or 16-bar chunks (depending on the selected checkpoint) and embedded in the latent space. Out of this embedding vectors a style vector will be calculated (mean). This style vector (hopefully) represents your style. The melody can then be steered with the style control slider.
