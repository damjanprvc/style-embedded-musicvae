StyleVAE - An AI powered composing tool for realtime human-AI co-creation.

How to use?

1. Start with an initial melody by either a) sampling a random sequence from the model or by b) uploading your own melody to use it as a staring point.
2. Now the melody can be modified or steered by tweaking the 'Style Control Slider'. Each slider (hopefully) adds more of its style characteristics to the current melody.
3. You can upload your personal melodies to create personal style slider. Click the '+ Button' and upload your own monophonic melodies (preferable in C Maj or A Min) to let the AI extract your style.
4. Play the melody sequence with the 'Play Button'.

The 'Randomness Slider' controls the amount of randomness i.e. how random the notes will be chosen by the AI. Lower values result in more conservative notes, while higher values result in more unconventional notes being chosen.
'Resample' and 'Sample Similar' buttons create slight variations of the current melody.
You can change the AI model checkpoint (which mainly sets the length of the melody sequence) and choose between many different instrument samples for the playback.

With the additional control you can output how the AI defines each style as a melody sequence.
